Black Jack

FREE CHIPS – Play the game every day to get free chips! Blackjack, also known as "21", is a casino card game where players compete against the dealer to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over 21. Players receive all cards face up, while the dealer's first card is face up and the second is face down. Picture cards (Jack, Queen, King) each count as 10, and aces count as 1 or 11. If a hand goes over 21, it is called a “bust” or “break” and the wager is lost.
